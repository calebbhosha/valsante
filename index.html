ABOUT US

Valerie Chigunde

Chief Executive Officer

Valerie Chigunde is The Founder & CEO of Valsante’ Organics. Valsante’ was first created out of necessity as an at home self-care package that was non-chemically formulated. Valerie worked with Trichologists, Dermatologists and Pharmacists to create this line.


With a background in Marketing and Customer Care, Valerie created specific products that are customer centric offering one-on-one hair consultations to understand customer needs and desires as well as offering platforms for product knowledge. As a naturalist herself, Valerie became the first client and model for the products.
